Screens

1

Search Screen

Live search as you type. Results grouped by Top / Songs / Albums / Artists / Videos. Enter to play, a to queue, l for album context.

2

Library Browser

Tabs for Songs, Albums, Artists, Playlists, Liked. Scrollable list views. Enter drills into album/artist. Space plays from selection.

3

Playlist View

Two-pane: left = playlist list, right = selected playlist tracks. a to add all to queue. d to remove track.

4

Queue Manager

Ordered list of upcoming tracks. Ctrl+Up/Down to reorder. d to remove. Shows currently playing at top. Auto-advances.

5

Now Playing (Full)

Expanded view: large album art (ascii/ansi), track details, progress bar with time, volume indicator, shuffle/repeat toggles.
